Market Context

Agriculture remains the economic backbone, yet the sector is fragmented. Farmers are battling systemic inefficiencies:

Climate & Resource VariabilityUnpredictable weather patterns and water scarcity impacting yields.
Economic InstabilityLimited access to fair market channels leads to income volatility.
Technological GapHigh demand for data-driven productivity tools that remain inaccessible.
